WEB SERVICE
ActionMap

I developed a RESTful full‑stack web service for voters to learn about and connect with their political progressive community and applied MVC design pattern and SOLID principles.

I performed BDD testing and debugging with Cucumber, continuous integration with Travis CI, and test coverage with CodeCov.

Following Agile and as student lead, I planned daily scrums and led retrospectives to reflect on team's progress. I also used Pivotal Tracker to track the project's user stories.
